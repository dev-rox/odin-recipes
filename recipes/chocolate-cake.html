Chocolate Cake

How to make the perfect Chocolate Cake

This is a really easy yet exceptional Chocolate Cake that truly tastes of chocolate. The crumb is beautifully tender and moist, and it keeps for up to 5 days. It requires no beater, just a wooden spoon.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Warm water
  • Chocolate powder
  • Wheat
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Beat eggs with sugar until doubled in size.
  2. Mix oil and cocoa powder in warm water.
  3. Gradually place 1 spoon of the liquid mixture and a spoon of wheat in the egg with the sugar already beaten, until it runs out.
  4. After getting a homogeneous mass put 1 spoon of yeast and mix lightly. Put it on a tray and bake in the oven at 180 degrees for 40 minutes, if you like brigadeiro on top, just put it after roast.
