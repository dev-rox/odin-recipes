How to make the perfect burger

I know you know how to make a cheeseburgerâ€¦ but as it turns out, I have some tips you might find useful! From the best beef to the best bun to the best cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Ground meat
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Bread
  • Lettuce Leaf
  • Tomato Slices
  • Salt
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Prepare a plate, and place a slice of bread in a pan.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat, without oil and place the hamburger.
  3. Fry until golden.
  4. Once fried, place the hamburger on the bun.
  5. With the skillet still on the fire, add a little oil and then the egg, add salt to taste.
  6. When the egg is fried, place it over the hamburger and turn off the heat.
  7. Now just put the lettuce leaf on top, the tomato slices and cover with the other slice of bread
